SR9009 works by increasing the metabolic rate of the body. It binds to a protein called Rev-erbα, which in turn is responsible for regulating the glucose metabolism production of fat storing cells and the activity of macrophages (it controls the rate of removal of dead cells from body). Thus by associating with this very important protein, SR9009 increases the metabolic rate of skeletal muscles in the body. It also decreases the level of cholesterol and triglycerides.
